About Black Widow

  • Real Name: Natalia Alianovna Romanova / Наталья Альяновна "Наташа" Романов
  • Known as: Natasha Romanoff
  • Other alias: Natalie Rushman, Fanny Longbottom
  • Born in: Soviet Union
  • Date of Birth: December 3, 1984
  • Played by: Scarlett Johansson

Natasha Romanoff who is famously known as the "Black Widow", was born in the Soviet Union. She was orphaned as a child and was absorbed as a KGB Assassin in the “Red Room.” She left Russia at some point in her life which was when Nick Fury came into the picture, and she became one of his most trusted spies. She is also one of the original 6 of the Earth's Mightiest Heroes, the Avengers.

Knowledge & Skills

Natasha has been trained in espionage and hand-to-hand combat since she was a very young age which considers her to be one of the best assassins in the Marvel Universe. She uses her guns, widow bites, and dual batons for battles. Her knowledge in infiltration, weapons, and her way of gathering intelligence and data allows her to navigate difficult situations in where a normal person can't handle.

It was also shown in some cases that Natasha can manipulate certain situations or people to make them give her what she wants to know from them. In the movie "Avengers: Endgame", they also showed that Natasha was usually the one who handled the leadership aspect of the team after the snap.
